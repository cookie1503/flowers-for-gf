A SHORT POEM FOR THE CUTEST GIRL EVER!!!!!

In this garden of love, I wish to compile,

A verse of blooms for your beautiful smile,

Your beauty, a bouquet of nature's grace,

Each flower describing your beautiful face.


Prettiest in the world, a beauty so rare,

With eyes like orchids, so bright and fair,

Entered my life with a touch of rose on your smile,

Making me dance with joy inside.


Like a lily, pretty in spirit and soul,

With you my being feels whole,

Your laughter is intoxicating,

Painted like daffodil's hue.


Just like a blooming daisy,

Your cute smile drives me crazy,

Your soft lavender touch,

In your love I've found so much.


Your face as adorable as a lotus bloom,

With you,my darling, there is no gloom,

I can never get enough of your jasmine scent,

While we cuddle on our bed.


Just like a tulip,

Always so still and serene,

Babygirl there is no competition,

It's just us until the end of time.
